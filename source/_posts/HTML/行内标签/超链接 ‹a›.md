                    </li>
                    <li><span style="font-size:11.5pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">_blank:
                            </span></span><span style="font-size:11.5pt"><span

                            </span></span><span style="font-size:11.5pt"><span

                            </span></span><span style="font-size:11.5pt"><span
                                style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">相同。</span></span></li>
                    <li><span style="font-size:11.5pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">_top:

                            style="font-size:11.5pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">
                                parent</span></span><span style="font-size:11.5pt"><span
                                style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">）。如果没有</span></span><span
                            style="font-size:11.5pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;"> parent
                            </span></span><span style="font-size:11.5pt"><span
                                style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">框架或者浏览上下文，此选项的行为方式相同</span></span><span
