<ul style="list-style-type:disc">
    <li><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">HTML</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">中的</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">&lt;svg&gt;</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">标签允许内嵌</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">SVG</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">图形，它在</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">HTML 5</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">标准出现</span></span>
    </li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">&nbsp;</span></span></p>
<ul style="list-style-type:disc">
    <li><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">如果</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;"> svg </span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">不是根元素，</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">svg </span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">元素可以用于在当前文档（比如说，一个</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;"> HTML </span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">文档）内嵌套一个独立的</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;"> svg </span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">片段 。
                这个独立片段拥有独立的视口和坐标系统</span></span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">&nbsp;</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">&nbsp;</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">&nbsp;</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:13.5pt"><strong><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">什么是</span></strong><strong><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">SVG</span></strong></span></p>
<ul style="list-style-type:disc">
    <li><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">SVG </span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">指可伸缩矢量图形</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;"> (Scalable Vector
                Graphics)</span></span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">&nbsp;</span></span></p>
<ul style="list-style-type:disc">
    <li><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">SVG </span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">用于定义用于网络的基于矢量的图形</span></span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">&nbsp;</span></span></p>
<ul style="list-style-type:disc">
    <li><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">SVG </span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">使用</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;"> XML </span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">格式定义图形</span></span>
    </li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">&nbsp;</span></span></p>
<ul style="list-style-type:disc">
    <li><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">SVG </span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">图像在放大或改变尺寸的情况下其图形质量不会有损失</span></span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">&nbsp;</span></span></p>
<ul style="list-style-type:disc">
    <li><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">SVG </span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">是万维网联盟的标准</span></span>
    </li>
</ul>
