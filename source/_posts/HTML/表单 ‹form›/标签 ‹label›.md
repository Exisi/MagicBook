<ul style="list-style-type:disc">
    <li><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">&lt;label&gt;
            </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">标签表示用户界面中某个元素的说明，用于关联</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;"> &lt;input&gt;
            </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">标签，一个</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;"> input </span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">可以与多个标签相关联</span></span>
    </li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">&nbsp;</span></span></p>
<ul style="list-style-type:disc">
    <li><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">将一个</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;"> &lt;label&gt;
            </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">和一个</span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;"> &lt;input&gt;
            </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">元素相关联主要有这些优点：</span></span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">&nbsp;</span></span></p>
<ol style="list-style-type:decimal">
    <li value="1"><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">标签文本不仅与其相应的文本输入元素在视觉上相关联，程序中也是如此。
                这意味着，当用户聚焦到这个表单输入元素时，屏幕阅读器可以读出标签，让使用辅助技术的用户更容易理解应输入什么数据。</span></span></li>
</ol>
<p><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">&nbsp;</span></span></p>
<ol style="list-style-type:decimal">
    <li value="2"><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">你可以点击关联的标签来聚焦或者激活这个输入元素，就像直接点击输入元素一样。这扩大了元素的可点击区域，让包括使用触屏设备在内的用户更容易激活这个元素。</span></span>
    </li>
</ol>

<p style="margin-left: 40px;"><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span
