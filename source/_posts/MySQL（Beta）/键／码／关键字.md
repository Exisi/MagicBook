<p><span style="font-size:13.5pt"><strong><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">超级</span></strong><strong><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">-</span></strong><strong><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">键</span></strong> <strong><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">/ </span></strong><strong><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">码</span></strong><strong><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;"> / </span></strong><strong><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">关键字</span></strong></span></p>
<ul style="list-style-type:disc">
    <li><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">超级键是一个或多个属性的集合，这些属性可以让我们在一个实体集中唯一地标识一个实体。即</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><u><span
                    style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">所有的键都是超级键</span></u></span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">&nbsp;</span></span></p>
<ul style="list-style-type:disc">
    <li><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">如果</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">K</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">是一个超级键，那么</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">K</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">的任意超集也是超级键，也就是说如果</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">K</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">是超级键，那么所有包含</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">K</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">的集合也是超级键。即同时</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><u><span
                    style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">包含键</span></u></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">的集合都是超级键</span></span>
    </li>
</ul>
