                style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">子句是空的，它将整个查询行集视为一个分区。窗口函数因此产生一个全局和，但对每一行都这样做。</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;"></span></span><br></li>
</ul>
<ul style="list-style-type: disc; margin-left: 40px;">
    <li><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">第二个</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;"> OVER </span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">子句按国家划分行，产生每个分区（每个国家）的总和。该函数为每个分区行生成此总和。</span></span>
    </li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">&nbsp;</span></span></p>
<ul style="list-style-type:disc">
    <li><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">仅在选择列表和</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;"> ORDER BY </span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">子句中允许使用窗口函数。查询结果行由</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;"> FROM </span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">子句确定，在</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;"> WHERE</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">、</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">GROUP BY </span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">和</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;"> HAVING </span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">处理之后，窗口执行发生在</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;"> ORDER BY</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">、</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">LIMIT </span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">和</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;"> SELECT DISTINCT
            </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">之前。</span></span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">&nbsp;</span></span></p>
<ul style="list-style-type:disc">
    <li><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">许多聚合函数都允许使用</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;"> OVER </span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">子句，因此可以将其用作窗口或非窗口函数，具体取决于是否存在</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;"> OVER </span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">子句</span></span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">&nbsp;</span></span></p>
<ul style="list-style-type:disc">
    <li><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">作为这些非聚合窗口函数之一的示例，此查询使用</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">
                ROW_NUMBER()</span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">，它生成其分区内每一行的行号。在这种情况下，行按国家</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">/</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">地区编号。默认情况下，分区行是无序的，行编号是不确定的。</span></span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">&nbsp;</span></span></p>
<ul style="list-style-type:disc">
    <li><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">要对分区行进行排序，请在窗口定义中包含</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;"> ORDER BY </span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">子句。该查询使用无序和有序分区（</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">row_num1 </span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">和</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;"> row_num2 </span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span
                style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">列）来说明省略和包含</span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;"> ORDER BY </span></span><span
            style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;">之间的区别：</span></span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;">&nbsp;</span></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:12.0pt"><span style="font-family:&quot;Comic Sans MS&quot;"><span
