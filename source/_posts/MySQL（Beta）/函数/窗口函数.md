     style='font-family:"Comic Sans MS";font-size:12.0pt'> ORDER BY </span><span
     style='font-family:"Microsoft YaHei UI";font-size:12.0pt'>子句中允许使用窗口函数。查询结果行由</span><span
     style='font-family:"Comic Sans MS";font-size:12.0pt'> FROM </span><span
     style='font-family:"Microsoft YaHei UI";font-size:12.0pt'>子句确定，在</span><span
     style='font-family:"Comic Sans MS";font-size:12.0pt'> WHERE</span><span

     style='font-family:"Comic Sans MS";font-size:12.0pt'> HAVING </span><span

</ul>
